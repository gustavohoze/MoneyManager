## 2. Information We Collect

MoneyManager may process the following categories of data:

- App usage analytics:
  - Event name
  - Event timestamp
  - Session identifier
  - User identifier used by the app
  - Event properties relevant to feature usage
- Financial records you enter in the app:
  - Transactions, categories, budgets, accounts, and related metadata
- Security and device-level permissions:
  - Face ID preference and lock state
  - Notification permission state and notification scheduling metadata

## 3. How We Use Information

We use data to:

- Provide core app functionality (tracking expenses, budgets, and insights)
- Sync app data across your devices when iCloud/CloudKit is enabled
- Improve reliability and features using aggregated analytics
- Deliver reminders and notifications when you enable them
- Protect access to your data when you enable Face ID lock

## 4. Data Storage and Processing

- Local app data is stored on your device.
- If iCloud sync is enabled, data is processed via Apple CloudKit under your iCloud account.
- Analytics events are processed by Supabase infrastructure configured for this app.

## 5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information.

Data may be shared only with service providers needed to operate the app:

- Apple (CloudKit/iCloud services and platform services)
- Supabase (analytics infrastructure)

## 6. Tracking

MoneyManager is not designed to track users across third-party apps or websites for advertising purposes.

## 7. Your Choices

You can:

- Disable notifications in iOS Settings
- Disable Face ID lock in app settings
- Disable iCloud sync by signing out of iCloud or changing iCloud settings
- Stop using the app and remove local data by deleting the app (subject to iCloud backup/sync behavior)

## 8. Data Retention

- Local data is retained until you delete it or uninstall the app.
- iCloud/CloudKit retained data follows Apple account and cloud retention behavior.
- Analytics retention follows your Supabase project retention configuration.

## 9. Children

MoneyManager is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## 10. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect data. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

## 11. International Transfers

Depending on your location and service infrastructure, data may be processed in countries other than your own.

## 12. Changes to This Policy
